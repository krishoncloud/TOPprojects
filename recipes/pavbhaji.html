Pav Bhaji

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup green peas, boiled
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp pav bhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon and cilantro for garnish
  1. Heat butter in a large pan and sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Add bell pepper and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Mix in pav bhaji masala, boiled peas, and mashed potatoes. Cook for 10 minutes, mashing everything together.
  4. Add water to adjust consistency, simmer, and serve with buttered pav, lemon, and cilantro. Enjoy!

Pav Bhaji is a spicy mashed vegetable curry served with soft buttery bread. It's perfect for dinner or a hearty snack. Simply cook vegetables with spices, mash them, and enjoy with toasted pav and a squeeze of lemon.
