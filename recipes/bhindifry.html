Bhindi Fry

Ingredients

  • 250g bhindi (okra), washed and chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and green chilies, and saut√© until onions are golden.
  3. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, and chopped bhindi. Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  4. When bhindi is tender and not sticky, sprinkle garam masala, adjust salt, and serve hot.

Bhindi Fry is a simple and flavorful side dish made with stir-fried okra and Indian spices. It's easy to prepare and pairs perfectly with chapati or rice.
