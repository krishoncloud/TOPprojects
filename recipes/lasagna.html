To make a quick lasagna, start by boiling 9 lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside. In a pan, cook 1 lb of ground beef or Italian sausage until browned, then stir in 2 cups of marinara sauce, 1 tsp each of garlic powder and dried oregano, plus salt and pepper to taste, and let it simmer for 5 minutes. In a bowl, mix 1 cup ricotta cheese, 1 cup shredded mozzarella, 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, and 1 egg until smooth. In a baking dish, layer a thin layer of meat sauce, followed by 3 noodles, a layer of cheese mixture, and repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with meat sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella on top.
